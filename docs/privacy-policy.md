# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: November 25, 2025**

## Introduction

JECRC Dating ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our dating platform exclusively for JECRC University students.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information
- **Email Address**: Your @jecrc.ac.in email for verification and communication
- **Display Name**: Your chosen name to display on your profile
- **Profile Information**: Bio, interests, gender, department, year of study
- **Photos**: Profile pictures and verification selfies
- **Date of Birth**: For age verification (must be 18+)

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Browser type, device type, IP address
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform
- **Location Data**: General location (not precise GPS)
- **Cookies**: Session tokens, preferences

### User-Generated Content
- **Messages**: Chat messages with matches
- **Likes/Passes**: Your preferences and interactions
- **Reports**: Content you report for moderation

## How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:
- **Provide Services**: Match you with compatible students, enable chat
- **Verify Identity**: Ensure only JECRC students can access the platform
- **Improve Platform**: Analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, add features
- **Safety & Moderation**: Review reports, prevent abuse, enforce guidelines
- **Communication**: Send notifications, updates, and important announcements
- **Compliance**: Meet legal obligations and protect rights

## Information Sharing

We **DO NOT** sell your personal information. We may share information with:

### Service Providers
- Cloud hosting (AWS, etc.)
- Email services (SendGrid, etc.)
- Analytics (anonymized data only)

### Legal Requirements
- Law enforcement (with valid legal request)
- Court orders or subpoenas
- Protection of rights and safety

### University Officials
- With your consent or as required by university policy
- Aggregate statistics only (no personal data)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your data
- **Correction**: Update inaccurate information
- **Deletion**: Request account and data deletion
- **Portability**: Export your data
- **Opt-out**: Disable notifications (except essential ones)
- **Object**: Contest automated decisions

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@jecrc.ac.in

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption**: SSL/TLS for data in transit, encryption at rest
- **Authentication**: Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions, admin monitoring
- **Regular Audits**: Security assessments and penetration testing
- **Backup**: Regular encrypted backups

However, no system is 100% secure. Report security concerns to security@jecrc.ac.in.

## Photo Moderation

All photos undergo:
- Automated content moderation (ML-based)
- Manual review by trained moderators
- Rejection of inappropriate content (nudity, violence, etc.)

Verification selfies are reviewed by admins and deleted after approval/rejection.

## Data Retention

We retain your data:
- **Active Accounts**: While your account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: 30 days (for recovery), then permanently deleted
- **Messages**: Deleted with match/account deletion
- **Moderation Logs**: 1 year for safety/legal purposes
- **Analytics**: Anonymized data indefinitely

## Children's Privacy

You must be **18 years or older** to use JECRC Dating. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we discover a user under 18, we will delete their account immediately.

## University-Specific Provisions

- **Email Verification**: Your @jecrc.ac.in email is verified to ensure you're a current student
- **Graduation**: Access may be revoked after graduation (per university policy)
- **Code of Conduct**: Usage subject to JECRC's student code of conduct
- **Campus Safety**: We cooperate with campus security for safety concerns

## Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Review their policies before sharing information.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via:
- Email notification
- In-app announcement
- Consent requirement for continued use

## International Users

Our services are designed for JECRC University students in India. If you access from outside India, your data may be transferred to servers in India or other locations.
